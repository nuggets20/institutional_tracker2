The Process

From Filing to Conviction

01
Ingest SEC 13F Filings
Quarterly 13F filings from 500+ institutions are pulled directly from SEC EDGAR. Every position, change in shares, and new/exited holding is captured and normalized.
02
Run 7 Signal Engines
Each signal engine processes the data independently — sector rotation, fund clustering, momentum acceleration, early accumulation detection, and more — producing per-stock scores.
03
Composite Scoring
Signal scores are weighted and combined into a single Conviction Score. Stocks that rank across multiple signals simultaneously are flagged as highest-priority opportunities.
04
Dashboard & Review
Results surface in an interactive master dashboard. Each signal has its own view, and the composite output gives a ranked shortlist of the most compelling setups right now.
